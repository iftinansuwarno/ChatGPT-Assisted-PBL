# Instructional Prompt Library: ChatGPT-Assisted PBL

This library contains the specific prompts used in the **"Human-in-the-Loop"** architecture. These prompts are designed to facilitate cognitive scaffolding rather than direct answer retrieval.

---

## Phase 1: Problem Orientation (The Hook)
**Goal:** To simplify complex biological contexts and spark curiosity.

**Prompt:**
> "I am an 11th-grade student studying the human excretory system. My current case study involves a teenager with sudden lower back pain and hematuria. Act as a diagnostic assistant and provide 5 potential biological causes for these symptoms. Explain each cause using a simple analogy suitable for my age group."

---

## Phase 2: Independent Investigation (Research Assistant)
**Goal:** To understand molecular mechanisms and deep biological processes.

**Prompt:**
> "Explain the role of the **glomerulus** in blood filtration. Specifically, how does chronic high blood pressure lead to permanent damage in this structure? Use professional biological terminology but provide a summary paragraph that is easy to understand."

**Critical Follow-up Task:** 
*Students are required to verify the AI's explanation against their textbook (e.g., Campbell Biology) to ensure the 'anatomical accuracy' of the filtration process.*

---

## Phase 3: Solution Development (Brainstorming Partner)
**Goal:** To apply learned theories into creative public health interventions.

**Prompt:**
> "Our team is designing a digital poster to prevent kidney disease among high schoolers. We want to focus on 'hidden sugars' in energy drinks. Give us 3 catchy slogans and 2 creative visual metaphors that compare a healthy kidney to a cleaning system."

---

## Phase 4: Evaluation & Reflection (Critical Review)
**Goal:** To identify AI limitations and build information evaluation skills.

**Prompt:**
> "Review the previous information you provided about Hematuria. Is there any recent medical consensus (post-2023) that might add more depth to this topic, or any common misconceptions that AI often generates about kidney filtration that I should be aware of?"

---

## Usage Guidelines
1. **Prompt Transparency:** Students must record these prompts in their "Prompt Log".
2. **The 2-Source Rule:** No AI-generated claim is accepted unless cross-referenced with at least two external academic sources.
